This guide is useful for mouning an arch-installed disk via a live arch USB.
Confirm your partitions:
lbslk -f

Mount subvolumes and boot:
mount {root partition} /mnt -o subvol=@
mount {esp partition} /mnt/boot/efi

(Optional) Mount home subvolume:
mount {root partition /mnt/home -o subvol=@home

Run arch-chroot
arch-chroot /mnt

For further information, see
https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Chroot#Running_on_Btrfs
